StickyScroll Issue

This is a super-basic demo to show the limitations of the onscroll event. The left sidebar is supposed to follow you as you scroll down the page smoothly and without fixed positioning, but it seems that the onscroll event doesn't fire enough to make that a possibility. The reason we avoid fixed positioning is to be able to support mobile and tablet devices and fluid layouts.

  • Support for mobile/tablet devices
  • Support for fluid layouts
